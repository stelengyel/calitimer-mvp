4. Tap the gear icon during a session. Toggle off "Detection Indicator". Confirm the dot disappears from the camera overlay.

6. Hold for 65 seconds. Timer should show "1:05" (not "65"). After hold ends, timer freezes on the final time.

**DETE-06 — Target alert:**
7. In the gear sheet, set target to 5s. Hold a 7-second handstand. Confirm: (a) a single beep fires at ~5s, (b) no second beep, (c) timer text turns green at the 5s mark.

**DETE-07 — On-the-fly target change:**
8. Open the gear sheet DURING an active hold. Change the target. Dismiss sheet. Confirm the new target takes effect for the next hold without ending the session.
