## Decisions Made

- `CameraActor` uses `@globalActor` (not `@MainActor`) because `AVCaptureSession.startRunning()` is a blocking call — executing it on MainActor would freeze the UI.
- `previewLayer` is `nonisolated let` — `AVCaptureVideoPreviewLayer` is not `Sendable`, so `nonisolated let` is the correct mechanism to safely cross the actor boundary to SwiftUI's `@MainActor` layer.
- `permissionDenied` is `@MainActor var` — enables direct `@State` binding in SwiftUI without requiring `MainActor.run` call-site boilerplate.
- Session preset `hd1920x1080` chosen for Phase 4+ Vision pose detection suitability; 4K omitted due to thermal overhead not being justified for a preview-only phase.
